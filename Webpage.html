Political Influence of Performers

Music performers and society exist in a constant cycle of influence. Major social events often shape the political beliefs that performers develop, giving them the personal and cultural foundation for what they choose to express through music. At the same time, performances can raise the visibility of political issues by turning abstract social problems into emotional and influential debates. Through performences (including lyrics, stage presence, audience interaction, etc.) performers help sharpen the meaning of a political topic and make it easier for the public to recognize, discuss, and remember. Audience feedback also plays an important role in this relationship. The reactions performers receive can influence what they notice, what they believe, and what they decide to say next. In which, the cycle continues.

Great Depression

A worldwide economic crisis that began in 1929 and caused unemployment, poverty, and insecurity.

Wikipedia
Woody Guthrie is affected by the Great Depression because economic hardship shaped the subjects and tone of his music.

Dust Bowl Migration

Severe drought and dust storms forced many families to leave the Great Plains during the 1930s.

Wikipedia
Woody Guthrie is affected by the Dust Bowl migration because he saw displaced workers and migrants as part of his audience and subject.

World War II

A global war from 1939 to 1945 that changed ideas about patriotism, labor, and public responsibility.

Wikipedia
Woody Guthrie participated in World War II by connecting his music and public image to anti-fascist politics.

Civil Rights Era

A broad movement for racial equality, voting rights, and legal protections in the United States.

Wikipedia
Marvin Gaye is affected by the Civil Rights era because racial injustice shaped the political concerns he later expressed in music.

Vietnam War

A long Cold War conflict that created intense debate, protest, and grief in American society.

Wikipedia
Marvin Gaye is affected by the Vietnam War because the war shaped the questions of violence, loss, and responsibility in his political music.

Hurricane Maria

A 2017 hurricane that caused major destruction and a humanitarian crisis in Puerto Rico.

Wikipedia
Bad Bunny is affected by Hurricane Maria because Puerto Rico's recovery and government response became part of his political context.

Puerto Rico Protests

Mass protests in 2019 demanded accountability from Puerto Rican leadership.

Wikipedia
Bad Bunny participated in the Puerto Rico protests by using his platform to support public anger and political accountability.

2024 Presidential Election

The U.S. presidential election between Kamala Harris and Donald Trump.

Wikipedia
Bad Bunny participated in the 2024 presidential election by sharing Kamala Harris's post about Puerto Rico with his Instagram audience.
